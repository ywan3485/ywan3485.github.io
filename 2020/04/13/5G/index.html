5G?

Posted on 2020-04-13 |

Do I oppose 5G?
Even while 5G poses absolutely no physical threat to the human body, I still oppose its rollout (https://www.theguardian.com/…/5g-safe-radiation-watchdog-he…).
My reasons are as follows:

  • Large-scale technological leaps in Capitalism are often the result of, or accompanied by, a decrease in the relative power of the working class. For example, in the 19th century there was a secretive group of workers called ‘the Luddites’. The Luddites sought to sabotage the new textile machinery of early Capitalism. The reason was that these machines completely overrode the skill set of the workers leading to a decrease in their bargaining position.
  • 5G will be followed by a transition to automation of the gig economy. Driverless cars and delivery drones are examples which spring to mind. If we were in Socialism, some of this might be welcomed as it wouldn’t disempower Uber drivers for example. But in Capitalism, technological leaps are primarily directed at raising the competitive stakes between the ruling class, rather than for the good of the working class.
  • 5G will enable the rapid enlargement of the Internet of Things. This is a transition in the internet where mundane devices such as fridges and doorbells are connected to the internet - who the fuck knows why. While this might be #Really #Cool from an 8yr old’s perspective, it is actually extremely dangerous at this point in time. I have read technical advisories warning the cybersecurity community that the ‘Internet of Things’ devices are extremely vulnerable as they are often left without the security protections which have been developed for ordinary devices (for the technically minded, stack overflows on IoT devices are trivially exploitable due to a lack of ASLR, Canaries, or N^X). One particularly exciting example of this is that some models of 3D printers which are connected to the Internet can be hacked and heated up remotely in order to start literal fires (https://www.coalfire.com/…/With-IoT-Common-Devices-Pose-New…)
  • With decreased latency from increased transmission speed, the capacity for mass-surveillance will be unbelievable. Every second of every day, real-time data will be recorded on a scale and at a rate never before seen in human society. You can bet that this, since it will be in the hands of the ruling class and their Intelligence agencies, will be used against workers organisation.
    So while 5G is scientifically proven to NOT cause physical damage to the human body, I still oppose it. But only on the principle that the working class should have democratic control over the technologies which mediate our lives.
    But listen everyone. We need to oppose things for the RIGHT reasons. Otherwise we will put ourselves others at risk. I.e. through believing that coronavirus is a hoax to get 5G installed.
